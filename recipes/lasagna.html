Lasagna Recipe

Description

A tasty and satisfying dish, yet very easy to prepare using our list of mandatory ingridients and following the steps below.

Ingridients

  • 500g of gouda cheese,
  • 500g of pasta of your choice,
  • 100g of tomatoe sauce,
  • 300g of minced meat of your choice,
  • 50ml of olive oil.

Steps

  1. Prepare your oven, set the temperature at around 180 degrees and let it heat up,
  2. Boil the pasta of your choice until it becomes al'dente,
  3. Season your meat with a set of garlic, pepper, and salt,
  4. Mix up your meat, boiled pasta, tomatoe sauce, and olive oil in a bowl until it is evenly mixed,
  5. Place your mixed ingridients inbetween cheese slices,
  6. Put your lasagna into oven and let it bake for around 20 minutes.

Voila

Your lasagna is now ready, enjoy your meal!
